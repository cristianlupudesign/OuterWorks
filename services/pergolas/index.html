Common questions

A few quick answers about pergolas.

Do pergolas only work in big gardens?

No. A compact pergola can help a smaller space feel more organised and intentional.

Can a pergola go over paving or decking?

Yes. Those are two of the most common combinations.

Are pergolas mostly decorative?

They look great, but they also help define how the garden is actually used.
