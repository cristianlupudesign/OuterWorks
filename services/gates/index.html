Common questions

A few quick answers about gates.

Can you fit just one gate?

Yes. A single gate job is no problem.

Do gates have to match the fencing?

Not always, but when the styles work together the whole finish feels more considered.

Can a gate job include small boundary repairs?

Yes. If the surrounding section needs attention, we can usually factor that in.
