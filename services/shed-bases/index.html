Common questions

A few quick answers about shed bases.

Can you do the base before the shed arrives?

Yes. That is often the cleanest way to stage the work.

Do bases matter for smaller sheds too?

Yes. Even a compact shed benefits from being level and properly supported.

Can the surrounding area be tidied at the same time?

Absolutely. Clearance and path work are common add-ons.
