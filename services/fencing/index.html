Straight answers

Good to know about fencing.

Concrete posts or timber posts?

Both have their place. Concrete posts won't rot and pair well with lap panels and gravel boards. Timber posts suit close board and slatted runs where the look matters. We'll recommend what fits your fence and ground at the survey — it's part of the fixed spec on your quote.

Can you match my neighbour's fence or the existing height?

Usually, yes. We'll measure the run and match the style and height where we can, and flag anything that affects it — a boundary line, a shared post, or a height that would need agreeing first. No surprises once we're on site.

Do you take the old fence away?

Yes — the old fence, posts, spoil and offcuts all leave with us, and it's already in your fixed price. We don't leave a skip on your drive and we leave the boundary cleaner than we found it.
