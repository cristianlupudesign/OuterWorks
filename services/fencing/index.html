Common questions

A few quick answers about fencing.

Can you repair only part of a fence run?

Yes. If the rest of the run is sound, we can repair or replace the weak sections instead of forcing a full new fence.

Do you replace old fence panels and posts?

Yes. We can remove the tired material and fit the new run so the finish feels clean and complete.

Can fencing be paired with a new gate?

Absolutely. Gates are one of the most common add-ons to a fence replacement.
