Sleeper Planters

Built around the way the space is actually used.

Sleeper planters help organise the garden. They frame edges, lift planting areas and can soften the look of fencing, paving or decking.

They work especially well when you want structure without turning everything into hard landscaping.

Common questions

A few quick answers about sleeper planters.

Do sleeper planters work in small gardens?

Yes. They are often a great way to make a compact space feel more organised.

Can planters be added after decking or paving?

Definitely. They are often a strong finishing layer after the main build work.

Are planters purely decorative?

They look good, but they also help structure planting and improve the layout.
