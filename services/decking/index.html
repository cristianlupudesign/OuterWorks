Common questions

A few quick answers about decking.

Can decking work in a smaller garden?

Yes. Compact gardens often benefit most because decking quickly creates a practical seating area.

Do you do simple square decks and shaped layouts?

Both. We can keep it straightforward or work around the space you have.

What pairs well with decking?

Pergolas, sleeper planters and a clearance or tidy-up beforehand are common pairings.
