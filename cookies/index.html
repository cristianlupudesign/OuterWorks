Cookies Policy

How cookies and third-party tools may be used on the Outerworks website.

Outerworks Last updated 5 April 2026 Website template page

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites. They can help a site remember useful information, understand visits and improve basic functionality.

How this site uses them

This website is designed to work with minimal friction. At the moment it may use only essential browser storage and any cookies or similar technologies required by the hosting platform or connected third-party services.

Forms and third parties

Once a live Formspree endpoint is connected, form handling may involve third-party processing. If additional tools such as analytics, maps, live chat or advertising are added later, this page should be updated to reflect that.

Managing cookies

You can usually control or remove cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect how parts of a website function.

Updates

If the website starts using additional cookies or tracking tools, this page will be updated.
