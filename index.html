Straight answers

Good to know.

How fixed is the price?

Fixed. We survey before we quote, then confirm a per-metre band in writing — that's what you pay. It only moves if you change the spec, and then we agree it first.

How soon can you start?

We give you a start date at survey and put it in writing. If we don't start when we said — for any reason that's on us — you take £100 off the bill for every day we're late.

Which areas do you cover?

We're based in Ruislip (HA4) and work across West and North-West London — Pinner, Harrow, Uxbridge, Northwood, Rickmansworth and the surrounding patch. Send your postcode to confirm.

Do you only do fencing?

No — fencing is our core, but we handle decking, pergolas, gates, sheds, paving and garden clearances too, so one team can sort the whole garden to one standard.

Ready for a straight price?

Send your postcode and a rough length. We'll come back the same working day with next steps and book your free survey.
