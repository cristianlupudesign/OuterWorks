Straight answers

Before you ask.

How fixed is the price, really?

Fixed. We survey before we quote, then confirm a per-metre band in writing — that's what you pay. It only moves if you change the spec, and then we agree it first.

What if the ground is bad?

We check for it at the survey, which is exactly why we survey before quoting. If we find something genuinely hidden while digging, we stop, show you, and agree it before carrying on — never a surprise on the invoice.

How soon can you start?

We give you a start date at survey and put it in writing. If we don't start when we said — for any reason on us — you take £100 off the bill for every day we're late.

Which areas do you cover?

Based in Ruislip (HA4), covering West and North-West London — Pinner, Harrow, Uxbridge, Northwood, Rickmansworth and the surrounding patch. Send your postcode to confirm.

Do you only do fencing?

Fencing is our core, but we also handle decking, pergolas, gates, sheds, paving and garden clearances — so one team can sort the whole garden to one standard.

Lock in your fixed price.

Send your postcode and a rough length. We'll come back the same working day, book your free survey, and put a straight price and a start date in writing.
