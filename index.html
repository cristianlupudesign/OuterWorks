Before you call

Quick answers to the common questions.

Most enquiries come with the same handful of worries. Here is how we handle them.

How quickly will I hear back?

Within the same working day if your message lands between 08:00 and 17:00, Monday to Friday. Evenings and weekends usually get a reply the next morning.

Is the site visit really free?

Yes. No call-out fee, no obligation, no sales pressure. You only pay when you agree the written quote and the work goes ahead.

Do you take on smaller jobs?

Happily. A single fence panel, one gate, a shed base or a clearance is just as welcome as a full garden. We will tell you honestly if it is not a job for us.

What postcodes do you cover?

We are based in West London and usually work across HA, UB, W and NW postcodes around Ruislip, Uxbridge, Harrow, Northolt and Ealing. Larger jobs travel farther out — send a postcode and we will tell you quickly.

Will I know exactly what I am paying for?

Yes. The written quote breaks out materials, labour and any prep work so nothing is hidden. Changes on the day get agreed in writing before any extra cost.

Can I send photos instead of describing it?

Please do. WhatsApp to 07348 580359 with a couple of photos and a postcode is the fastest route to a useful reply.
