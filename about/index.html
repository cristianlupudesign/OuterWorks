Our story

Built by three, run as one.

We started Outerworks as an equal three-way partnership, based in Ruislip (HA4). No head office, no salesman between you and the people doing the work — just the three of us, splitting the graft and the responsibility straight down the middle.

We built it on two things that shouldn't be rare in this trade but are: a straight price you can hold us to, and turning up when we said we would. Too many gardens across West London get half-done and abandoned. That's the gap we set out to close.

We'd rather be a bit dearer and right than cheap and gone. When all three of us put our name to a job, it gets built once, built properly, and left the way we'd want our own gardens left.

What we stand for

The way we work, every time.

These aren't slogans — they're the commitments the three of us hold each other to on every job we take on.

  • Fixed price, confirmed at surveyWe look at the ground and the run first, then put a per-metre price in writing. It only moves if you change the spec — and we agree it with you first.
  • Guaranteed start datesYou get a start date in writing at survey, and we plan our diary so we can keep it. Turning up when we said is the whole point.
  • We build it oncePosts set to last, lines run straight, fixings that won't bleed rust. We'd rather take the extra hour than come back to a job that's dropped.
  • Site left cleanAll waste taken away, ground swept, gate closed. We leave your garden tidier than we found it — not with a skip's worth of offcuts on the lawn.
  • Named materialsYou'll know exactly what's going in — the timber, the posts, the fixings — written on your quote, so there's no swapping cheaper stock on the day.
  • Backed for 12 monthsEvery job carries a 12-month workmanship guarantee. If something's on us, one of the three of us comes back and puts it right.
